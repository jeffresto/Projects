Heres a timeline of his DOOM-ed life:

  • Born in a Romani camp outside Haasenstadt, Latveria.
  • His mother (sorceress) was killed after making a deal with the demon Mephisto.
  • His father (doctor) was killed by the king of Latveria after failing to save his daughter.
  • He began to study magic and technology, and gained the opportunity to study in America, where he met Reed Richards.
  • He attempted to build a portal to hell to resurrect his mother but the device exploded and damaged his face.
  • He then joined a mysterious order which taught him secret knowledge, including metallurgy.
  • He used it to create a suit of power armor.
  • Unfortunately he had put on the armor's mask before it had adequately cooled and his face was severely burned.
  • From that moment on he has been known as Doctor Doom.
